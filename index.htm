Lesson 1 - Introduction to Search Engine Optimization

What is SEO?

SOE stands for ÒSearch Engine OptimizationÓ. Basically, it answers the question “When someone searches for something in a search engine. ie: Google? How well will it find what your looking for, how fast will it find it, and where will it display when presented to you? Ie: top, middle, bottom? Side?; All the answers to these questions is why designing and developing a website that maximizes optimization is so important. How important? That depends on you, your business, and why you created a website in the first place.

How do we achieve “Search Engine Optimization”?

Well, basically, there are a bunch of people that dedicated themselves to identifying the best way to achieve this goal. If you do some research on the topic, you will quickly realize that “Best Practices” may vary. So donÕt get stuck on being perfect, no website is.. but hey, letÕs do the best we can to make it as optimal as possible. WouldnÕt you agree? Below are some of the important concepts you should consider when designing/developing your website.

  • Step 1: Create unique and informative page titles in all of your webpages

  • Step 2: Use the meta tag to provide more descriptive information about your page

  • Step 3: Be careful how you structure your URLs

  • Step 4: Make your site easy to navigate

  • Step 5: Offer quality content and services.
